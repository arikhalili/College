What is the SAT?

The SAT is a standardized college entrance exam accepted by most schools and universities as one of the many factors used to admit new students each school year.


Register for the SAT!

Important Details about the SAT
Duration 3 hours and 50 minutes
Parts of the Test Math, Evidence-Based Reading and Writing, Optional Essay
Cost $54.50 ($43 without Essay)
Maximum Score 800 per section
1600 for whole test
